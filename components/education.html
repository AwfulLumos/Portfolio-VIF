About the University

The University of Nueva Caceres (UNC) is one of the oldest and most prestigious educational institutions in the Bicol Region, founded in 1948. Known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, UNC has been producing competent professionals and leaders for over seven decades.

The university offers comprehensive programs in various fields including Information Technology, Engineering, Business, Education, and Liberal Arts. With state-of-the-art facilities and a faculty composed of experienced educators and industry practitioners, UNC provides an environment that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and real-world application of knowledge.
